Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Print-on-demand is a printing technology and business process where items are not printed until an order is received. This means products are created one at a time after they're ordered, eliminating the need for inventory and reducing waste. It's perfect for custom designs and small-batch production.

For the best results, we recommend uploading high-resolution PNG files with transparent backgrounds, SVG vector files, or PDF files. For photographs and complex graphics, high-resolution JPG files (at least 300 DPI) also work well. If you have questions about your specific file, please contact our design team for assistance.

Our standard production time is 2-3 business days after your order is confirmed. Shipping times vary depending on your location and chosen shipping method. Domestic orders typically arrive within 3-5 business days after production, while international shipping can take 7-14 business days. Expedited options are available for faster delivery.

Yes! We offer tiered pricing for bulk orders. Discounts start at orders of 25+ items and increase with larger quantities. For wholesale partnerships or large-volume recurring orders, please contact our sales team directly to discuss custom pricing and special arrangements.

Since all items are custom-made, we generally don't accept returns unless there's a production defect or shipping damage. If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 7 days of receipt with photos of the issue. We'll gladly replace the item or provide a refund. For sizing issues with apparel, we recommend checking our detailed size charts before ordering.
